Remote Controls 2

Two physically identical rectangular bars become light and dark at the same time, but in some conditions they look as if they wink in alternation. The appearance of winking (alternating) or blinking (bars in sync) can be controlled by rectangles placed in the vicinity of the modulating bars: the bars blink when the rectangles are the same height but alternate when there is a difference of height between the bars and the rectangles.

INSTRUCTIONS

Move the slider until rectangles are no longer blinking in unison. Then submit your result.
